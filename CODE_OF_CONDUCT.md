# Code of Conduct

Yes Man is a game about being on each other's side. The community around it
holds to the same promise.

## The short version

1. **Be kind.** The joke is never at someone's expense.
2. **No spoilers without tags.** Let people find the secrets themselves.
3. **No harassment, hate speech, or spam.** Yes to people; no to that.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that builds a welcoming community:

- Warmth, patience, and good-faith help.
- Respecting differing experiences and skill levels.
- Gracefully accepting constructive feedback.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discriminatory language, personal
attacks, sharing others' private information, and persistent disruption.

## Scope

This applies in all project spaces — the repository, issues, pull requests, and
any official community channels (e.g., Discord) — and when representing the
project in public.

## Enforcement

Report concerns to the maintainers _(owner to add a contact email/handle)_.
Reports are handled confidentially. Maintainers may warn, remove content, or
ban as needed to keep the space kind.
