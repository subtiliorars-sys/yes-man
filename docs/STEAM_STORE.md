## Title

**Yes Man**

## Short Description (≤ 300 chars)

A warm, funny idle game about the quiet super-power of saying *yes*. Tap, build
a chorus of enthusiastic helpers, and spread Cheer across the world. No timers,
no FOMO, full offline progress. Collect stamps, hunt secrets, and feel a little
lighter. Yes to you. 💛

## About This Game (long description)

Some games want your stress. **Yes Man** wants your shoulders to drop an inch.

You tap one big, friendly **YES** button. Cheer happens. Then you recruit an
Enthusiastic Dog ("YES to walks! YES to treats! YES to that squirrel!"), a
Supportive Friend who backs your worst ideas, an Automated Optimist, and
eventually the literal Cosmic Yes — a domino chorus of helpers who keep the
Cheer flowing while you live your life.

Between taps, life asks you small questions. *Help a friend move a couch? Try
the weird new restaurant? Reply to that email you've been dodging?* Say yes.
See what happens. (Your back will remember the couch. It was still worth it.)

**Yes Man is built like a gift, not a trap.** No countdown timers. No missable
daily logins. No loot boxes. Step away for a month and come back to a generous
pile of Cheer waiting for you. The prestige is optional and never punishing.
We sweated every frame of feel so you don't have to sweat anything at all.

### Features

- 🟡 **One-tap joy** — satisfying squash, particles, and a soft warm pop on
  every YES.
- 🐕 **A cast of enthusiastic helpers** — 7 auto-yesers, each with their own
  ridiculous, lovable voice.
- 📬 **35 "say yes to life" prompts** across three tiers — gentle, funny, and
  occasionally profound.
- ✨ **Golden Yeses** — surprise bubbles that drift by with a burst of Cheer.
  Miss one? Another's coming. No pressure, ever.
- 📖 **A Collection book** — 17 milestone stamps and 10 hidden secrets for the
  completionists who fall in love.
- 🌻 **A Fresh Outlook** — optional prestige that gently raises the whole
  world's Cheer.
- 🌙 **Honest idle** — full offline progress, costs shown upfront, your data
  exportable any time.
- ♿ **Kind to everyone** — Reduce Motion and Sound toggles, big readable UI,
  one-hand portrait play.

### Who it's for

Made for adults who want something cozy and clever after a long day — and
perfectly safe and delightful for the kids watching along. It's a game you can
play for five minutes or for thirty hours, and either way it's on your side.

> The whole game is really one small idea, taken seriously: saying *yes* is a
> tiny act with enormous, compounding consequences. Turns out that's also the
> exact shape of a good idle game.

Yes to good vibes. Yes to good craft. **Yes to you.**
