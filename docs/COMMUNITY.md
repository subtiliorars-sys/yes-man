# Yes Man — Community Resources & Scaffolding

> Everything needed to stand up a kind, healthy community. Owner creates the
> actual servers/accounts; this is the blueprint and the copy.

## Community Principles

The community is an extension of the game's promise: **be on each other's
side.** Warm, funny, generous. We moderate for kindness first.

## Discord Structure (proposed)

**Welcome**
- `#welcome` — auto-greet + the three rules (below).
- `#rules` — Code of Conduct summary + link.
- `#announcements` — patch notes, mirrors `CHANGELOG.md`.

**The Lounge**
- `#general` — hang out, share screenshots.
- `#say-yes` — players post real-life yeses they said. The heart of the server.
- `#cheer-counter` — flex your numbers, prestige bloom screenshots.

**Collectors**
- `#stamp-book` — milestone celebrations.
- `#secret-hunt` — *spoiler-tagged* secret hints. Hint culture, not answer
  dumps — preserve the joy of discovery.

**Support & Feedback**
- `#help` — saves, backups, devices. Pin the "how saves work" guide.
- `#feedback` — suggestions; tagged and triaged into GitHub issues.
- `#bug-reports` — template below.

**Off-topic**
- `#dogs` — non-negotiable. Every wholesome community needs a dog channel.

## The Three Rules (short Code of Conduct)

1. **Be kind.** The joke is never at someone's expense.
2. **No spoilers without tags.** Let people find the secrets themselves.
3. **No harassment, hate, or self-promo spam.** Yes to people, no to that.

Full CoC: adopt the Contributor Covenant for the repo
(`CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md` — owner to add) and a friendlier prose version for
Discord that links to it.

## Player FAQ (seed content)

**Will I lose my progress?** No. The game saves automatically and locally, and
you can Export a backup code any time from ⚙ Settings.

**Is anything online / am I being tracked?** No. Yes Man runs fully on your
device. Export/Import never touches a server.

**Did I miss a daily / limited event?** There are none. Nothing is missable.
Come back whenever; your Cheer kept accruing (up to 30 days offline).

**Do I have to prestige?** Never. "A Fresh Outlook" is optional and only ever
helps.

**The game is too quiet / too busy.** ⚙ Settings has **Sound** and **Reduce
Motion** toggles.

**How many secrets are there?** Ten. The Collection book shows your count.
That's all we'll say. 😊

## Light ARG / Secret-Hunt Kit (for launch engagement)

- Tease the *count* of secrets, never the triggers.
- Drip one cryptic hint per week in `#secret-hunt` (e.g., "old games remember
  old codes").
- Celebrate the first community member to find each one.

## Creator / Streamer Pack

- Keys with no embargo strings.
- A one-pager of spoiler-free talking points (see `docs/PRESS_KIT.md`).
- Permission to monetize videos freely.

## Support Escalation

Discord `#help` → tag maintainer → GitHub issue if reproducible. Target: every
first-week message gets a human reply within 24h.
