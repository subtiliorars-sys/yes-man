# Yes Man — Go-To-Market Plan

> Working plan for a calm, honest launch. Owner approves spend, dates, and the
> public store page (see `GOVERNANCE.md`).

## Positioning

**The idle game that refuses to manipulate you.** Cozy, funny, generous. The
anti-FOMO incremental. One line: *"Yes to good vibes. Yes to good craft."*

### Comparables (for tone & pricing, not cloning)

- Cozy/wholesome incrementals and "one more tap" comfort games at the $3–5 PC
  tier with free, ad-light mobile companions.
- We differentiate on **writing quality + honesty**: no timers, no ads gating
  progress, exportable saves.

## Pre-Launch (T-minus 8 weeks)

1. **Steam page live + wishlists** (the single most important lever). Use copy
   in `docs/STEAM_STORE.md`. Wishlists drive launch-day visibility.
2. **Capsule art + 6 screenshots + a 30–45s trailer.** Trailer beats below.
3. **Short devlog thread** on the "honest idle" philosophy — earns goodwill in
   incremental/cozy communities.
4. **Creator list:** cozy-game and idle-game YouTubers/streamers; offer keys
   with zero embargo strings. Lean on the secrets as discovery hooks.

## Trailer Beats (30–45s)

1. (0–4s) Black → one big YES tap, squash + pop. "Some games want your stress."
2. (4–12s) Helpers cascade in; Cheer counter spins up. "Yes Man wants your
   shoulders to drop."
3. (12–22s) Prompt cards flash by (the funny ones). A Golden Yes drifts past
   and bursts.
4. (22–32s) Stamp book / secrets reveal. "No timers. No FOMO. Full offline."
5. (32–40s) Logo + "Yes to you. 💛" + Wishlist now.

## Launch Week

- Ship green build (CI `verify` gate must pass).
- Launch discount: **10% off** for visibility without cheapening.
- Be present: answer every Steam discussion and Discord message in the first
  72 hours. Kindness is the brand.
- Pin a "How saves work / nothing is online / how to back up" post.

## Post-Launch (first 90 days)

- Patch cadence: small, frequent, well-noted (`CHANGELOG.md`).
- Free content drop ~Day 30: tier-4 prompts + a few new secrets (already
  scaffolded in `docs/ROADMAP.md`).
- Localization pass once strings settle (text is centralized for this).
- Begin mobile port track (`docs/MOBILE.md`).

## Channels & Cadence (sustainable, not spammy)

| Channel | Cadence | Content |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Steam page/news | Per patch | Honest patch notes, screenshots |
| Discord | Daily presence | Support, secret-hunt hints, polls |
| Short-form video | 1–2/week | Funniest prompts, "the dog," secrets |
| Devlog/blog | Monthly | Design philosophy, roadmap honesty |

## Success Metrics (north-star: goodwill, not whales)

- Wishlist → sale conversion; refund rate (target very low via honesty).
- Median session length and **return rate after 7 days** (offline-friendly
  design should shine here).
- Stamp/secret completion distribution (are collectors engaged?).
- Review sentiment: we are explicitly chasing "couldn't find a flaw."

## Hard "No"s (brand guardrails)

- No fake urgency, no countdown-to-buy timers, no ads that gate play.
- No real-money progression. Mobile tip jar is cosmetic only.
- Never punish a returning player. Ever.
